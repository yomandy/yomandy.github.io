04. About Me

My name is Mandy. I'm a Middle Eastern/Australian Product Marketing Manager trying to make my mark in the startup world. Before my foray into all things Fintech, I worked in video game marketing at Electronic Arts for five years. Even though I studied Social Inquiry at university, I landed in marketing by pure chance. I'm incredibly lucky to have the opportunities that I have had and my work has taken me all over the world.

Outside of work my hobbies come and go but at the moment I'm busy hyper-fixating on climbing, trying to grow a vegg0ie garden, and reading. Previously I have been known to skateboard, sail, and create digital art for fun.
